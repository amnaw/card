Persian Cat

A persian cat is quite an exquisite breed. They're also typically quiet and affectionate cats who enjoy being held.

  • Scientific name :Felis catus.
  • Average Lifespan: 10 to 15 years.
  • Average size : 7 to 12 pounds.
  • Origin :Iran(persia).
